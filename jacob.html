Jacob

Every day is the worst day of someone’s life.

At Lifeline they’re used to talking people down from the edge but when Cathy started her shift she never thought she’d have to stop a murder…

Tom’s in trouble. Someone has his daughter and they won’t let her go unless he robs a bank. At least that’s what he’s telling the cops…

And Jacob, well, Jacob is an odd one.
